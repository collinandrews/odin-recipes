Sweet & Sticky Barbecue Sauce

Description

Delicious barbecue that is perfect for braising pork!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup ketchup
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup apple juice
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper

Steps to Cook

  1. Mix all ingredients in bowl
  2. Apply sauce to your cooked meat of choice
  3. Enjoy!
